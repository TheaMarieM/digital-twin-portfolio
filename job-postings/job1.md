# Sample Job Posting - Senior Full Stack Developer

**Company**: TechCorp Australia  
**Location**: Melbourne, VIC (Hybrid - 3 days office, 2 days remote)  
**Salary**: $110,000 - $130,000 AUD + Super  
**Type**: Full-time, Permanent

## About the Role

We're seeking a Senior Full Stack Developer to join our growing engineering team. You'll be working on cutting-edge web applications that serve over 100,000 daily active users. This role offers the opportunity to work with modern technologies including React, Node.js, and cloud infrastructure.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and develop scalable web applications using React and Node.js
- Lead technical architecture decisions for new features and services
- Mentor junior developers and conduct code reviews
- Collaborate with product managers and designers on feature specifications
- Implement CI/CD pipelines and DevOps best practices
- Optimize application performance and scalability
- Participate in agile ceremonies and sprint planning

## Essential Requirements

- 5+ years of professional software development experience
- Expert-level proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript
- Strong experience with React and modern frontend frameworks
- Solid backend development skills with Node.js or Python
- Experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL)
- Understanding of RESTful APIs and microservices architecture
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
- Excellent communication skills and team collaboration

## Desirable Skills

- Experience with Docker and Kubernetes
- Knowledge of CI/CD tools (GitHub Actions, Jenkins, CircleCI)
- Familiarity with testing frameworks (Jest, Cypress, Pytest)
- Experience with Redis or other caching solutions
- Understanding of security best practices
- Experience mentoring or leading development teams
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field

## Key Selection Criteria

1. **Technical Excellence**: Demonstrated ability to build high-quality, scalable applications
2. **Leadership**: Experience mentoring developers and driving technical decisions
3. **Problem Solving**: Track record of solving complex technical challenges
4. **Communication**: Ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders
5. **Innovation**: Proactive approach to improving processes and technologies

## What We Offer

- Competitive salary package with performance bonuses
- Flexible hybrid work arrangement
- Professional development budget ($3,000/year)
- Latest MacBook Pro and equipment
- Health insurance and wellness programs
- 4 weeks annual leave + birthday leave
- Modern office in Melbourne CBD with free snacks and coffee

## Company Culture

TechCorp Australia values innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning. We're a team of 150+ people building products that make a real impact. Our engineering culture emphasizes code quality, automated testing, and knowledge sharing through regular tech talks and hackathons.

## Interview Process

1. **Phone Screen** (30 mins) - HR discussion about background and expectations
2. **Technical Interview** (1 hour) - Coding challenge and system design discussion
3. **Team Interview** (45 mins) - Meet potential colleagues and discuss collaboration style
4. **Final Interview** (30 mins) - Leadership team discussion about culture fit and career goals
